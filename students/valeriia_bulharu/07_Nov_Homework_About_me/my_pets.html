☻My pets photo

Kids talk about the funny things their pets do. This past Christmas Santa left me the best present ever, a puppy! As I open my last box with excitement, I decided to name her, Holly! I spent the whole Christmas day with her. Holly is full of energy and loves to play. One night when I was sleeping I heard this rumbling noise. I thought it was coming from outside but it wasn't. I look down at the floor and I see Holly's little tent shaking up and down. Holly was inside the tent playing and dancing. She made me laugh!
